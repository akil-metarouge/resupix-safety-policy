Resupix – Child Safety and CSAE Policy

Commitment to Child Safety

Resupix and Resupix Pty Limited are committed to protecting children from all forms of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content, conduct, or activity that involves or promotes child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, exploitation, or related harmful behavior.

We comply with all applicable child protection and safety laws in the jurisdictions where we operate, and we actively support global efforts to combat CSAE.

Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on Resupix:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form, including images, videos, text, or links.
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors.
  • Sharing, promoting, or requesting CSAM or CSAE content.
  • Impersonating a minor or misleading others for abusive purposes.

Violations of these standards will result in immediate suspension or removal of accounts, and where applicable, reports will be made to law enforcement authorities.

Reporting and Response

In-App Reporting

Users can report suspected CSAE content or behavior directly within the app using the Report option in the menu/settings/content screen. Reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.

Direct Contact

Users may also contact us at: Email: keryn.jensen@resupix.com

Enforcement and Cooperation

When Resupix obtains actual knowledge of CSAM:

  • We will immediately remove or block access to the material.
  • We will report confirmed CSAM to the appropriate authorities.
  • We will cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.

Compliance with Laws

We comply with applicable laws and regulations on child safety and child sexual abuse material in all jurisdictions where our app is distributed. Our procedures are aligned with Google Play’s Child Safety Standards.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect new laws, regulations, or best practices. The latest version will always be published at: https://resupix.com/child-safety-policy
